Who is Frank Ocean?

Lonny Breaux, better known by his stage name, Frank Ocean, is a singer, songrwiter, and producer who has become one of the most famous contemporary RnB artists of the past couple of years and has made huge hits like "Thinking Bout You", "Swim Good", and "Nights". His influential singing and lyricism has made waves all over the music industry. He first started making money by ghostwriting for many famous artists like Beyonce knowles and many more. Furthermore, Frank Ocean is known for being humble as well as a subdued online presence, which makes his music even more valuable. Not to mention, that many of his albums have been genre bending. For example, his first album, Channel Orange brought together funk, psychadelic , RnB, and soul sounds in order to create a beginning to a extremely fruitful career. Then, his sophomore album, blond brought his significant sound from the debut album, and expiremented with synths and a multitude of different melodies in his albums.
