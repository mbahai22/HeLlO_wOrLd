Who is Kid Cudi?

Scott Mescudi, known by his stage name of Kid Cudi, is a hip hop artist who started making music around the late 2000s along with other famous artists like the likes of Drake, the weeknd, and more. He is most famous for his early hits like "Day n' Nite", "Pursuit of Happiness", and "Erase Me". His first and most popular album, Man on the Moon: End of Day, is notorious for not only being instrumental in creating the distinct sound that Cudi would be known for, but it was also said to have been inspiring to many other artists in the modern day rap game; crediting Kid Cudi for inspiring them to make music and stay strong against mental health problems. This and many other aspects of Kid Cudi's music has solidified Cudi as one of the greatest legend's of the hip hop genre of all time
