Drake

Who Is Drake?

Aubrey Drake Graham is a Canadian hip hop artist who has been making music since the the late 2000s. Drake is most commonly known for his hit singles like "One Dance", "God's Plan", and "Hotline Bling". He has become one of the most popular and succesful artists of the past decade, blending different genres like Hip Hop, Rnb, Dancehall, and many more. Drake first came up on the scene with his mixtape, so far gone, an album in which he sampled different pop artists and made his own sound using passionate vocals mixed with clever bars.
