Who is the Weeknd?

Abel Tesfaye, better known by his stage name, The Weeknd, is a Canadian Ethiopian artist who became famous for his revolutionairy dark, experimental rnb sound that eventually came to inspire future artists. This sound can be most prominently heard in his first three mixtapes that later was brought together. The weeknd later become popular for his hit songs, "The Hills", "Starboy", and "Heartless".
